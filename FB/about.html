Our History & Legacy

Founded in 2021, SmileTech started with a vision to bridge the massive skill gap between college curriculum and industry demands. Over the years, we have empowered over 10,000+ students across various technology domains through professional internships, workshops, and certified programs.

From a single computer laboratory, we have grown into a multi-branch educational technology services company offering final-year project guidance, advanced paper publication assistance, and hardware-software prototyping labs.

Our Vision

To emerge as a premier technology learning ecosystem where engineering students acquire excellence, practical problem-solving capabilities, and high-tier industry placement readiness.

Our Mission

To deliver quality, industry-aligned certified training, expose students to emerging architectural setups, provide real-world hardware & software internships, and nurture innovations via active research and development setups.
